COVID-19 ANALYSIS

PYTHON

I analyzed the data from the Johns Hopkins University Center for Systems Science and Engineering to predict the number of cases in the coming months.

To do this, I used multiple techniques to create fitting predictions, including auto-regressive models and linear regression with polynomial basis functions to map out my predictions. I was then able to draw conclusions by examining the models; for example, by comparing the beta coefficients and root mean square errors, I was able to explain why the US’s exponential growth rate was higher than Italy’s.
